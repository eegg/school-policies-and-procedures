=======
Smoking
=======

.. include:: common-metadata.rst

.. contents::



Policy
------

Smoking is prohibited in all areas of The Setting, including the outdoor areas, unless otherwise designated.
Staff ensure this is observed at all times.


Procedures
----------


Awareness
^^^^^^^^^

* All staff, parents and volunteers are made aware of this policy.
* We display no-smoking signs.


Staff smoking during working hours
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Staff who smoke may only do so during working hours
if they are on a break and not in The Setting.
They make every effort to reduce the effect of the odour
and the effects of passive smoking for children and colleagues.


Legal framework
---------------

* health and safety regulations and the Welfare Requirements of the EYFS.
* `The Smoke-free (Premises and Enforcement) Regulations 2006 <www.opsi.gov.uk/si/si2006/20063368.htm>`_
* `The Smoke-free (Signs) Regulations 2007 <www.opsi.gov.uk/si/si2007/20070923.htm>`_
