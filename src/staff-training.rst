Policy
------

* We provide regular in-service training to all staff and volunteers.
* Our setting budget allocates resources to training.
* We support the work of our staff by holding regular supervision meetings and appraisals.


Procedures
----------


A staff member goes on a course
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

When staff member S goes on a course:

#. After returning to the nursery, S

   * cascades the information they gained on the course to all other staff
   * makes recommendations on amendments to practice in The Setting.
