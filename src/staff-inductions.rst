Procedures
----------


Induction procedure
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Prior to the first day of a new member of staff S,
|The Staff Inductions Officer| 


* Provides S with information detailing their job role:

  * A copy of the appropriate job description noted above,
    which should be signed and returned by the new staff member
  * A copy of the hierarchy of authority in the team,
    which must note his position therein

* introduces S to any Key Children they may be inheriting or having assigned to them, noting

  * Their name, age, sex, languages known, and other basic details
  * Where their records are kept

* introduces S to guardians, child collectors and clients,
  taking extra time with any allocated key children.

* introduces S to all current staff members by name, noting

  * What her role is in the team
  * How, or if, the new staff member will be working with her

* familiarises S with the building layout, noting

  * fire exits from every room
  * where resources, files, medication and equipment are kept

* directs S to information detailing his role:

  * our Policies and Procedures,
    which |The Staff Inductions Officer| advises him to read thoroughly,
    noting especially his job description and the Staff Handbook detailed therein.

* shows S how to

  * use electrical and cleaning equipment safely
  * complete planning for individual children and discussion times for all ages
  * observe and record a child's learning using written and photographed observations
  * prepare snacks and set out lunches safely
  * open and close the door to families
  * find and complete the register, medication, accident and incident and accidents at home files
  * complete a Learning Journey and Home School Diary

* shows S all of the Setting's Policies and Procedures.
  S will be asked to sign to state that he/she has read, understood and will abide by them.

* organises a small workshop regarding Safeguarding,
  after which S will receive a Safeguarding pack.

* organises a fire drill in which S will take part.
  
* gives S an induction sheet to complete with the first four weeks of attendance.

* organises for S to take online courses in

  * Safeguarding



Two weeks after a new member of staff has joined
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Two weeks after a new member of staff has joined,
|The Staff Inductions Officer|
organises a meeting to include

* |The Staff Inductions Officer|
* The new member of staff

in order to discuss

* Whether the new member of staff believes that
  he has settled into his role
* Whether there are any ambiguities or difficulties in
  what is being asked of him
* How well he has assimilated the Policies and Procedures
