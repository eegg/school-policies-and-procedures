Documents

Compulsory Items List

A list of items required to be with the child during their time at Lexden Montessori, specifying that all must be labelled with the name of the child, to include:

  • Underwear, plus spare
  • Polo neck T-shirt, plus spare
  • School coat
  • School bag
  • Black pumps
  • Wellington boots
  • Outdoor shoes
  • If male:
    • Grey socks, plus spare
    • Long shorts
    • Jumper
  • If female:
    • Grey tights, plus spare
    • Dress
    • Cardigan
  • If Summer:
    • Sun hat
    • Sun protection
  • If Winter:
    • Winter hat
    • Scarf
    • Mittens
  • If not dry:
    • Nappies
    • Wipes
    • Nappy sacks
  • If staying for lunch:
    • Lunch pack, ensuring that all items:
      • Are not on the Known Allergens List
      • Have ingredients listed on the packet; and failing that, have the ingredients accompanying them
    • Ice pack
