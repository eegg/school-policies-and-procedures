Policy

Smoking is prohibited in all areas of The Setting, including the outdoor areas, unless otherwise designated. Staff ensure this is observed at all times.

Procedures

Awareness

  • All staff, parents and volunteers are made aware of this policy.
  • We display no-smoking signs.

Staff smoking during working hours

Staff who smoke may only do so during working hours if they are on a break and not in The Setting. They make every effort to reduce the effect of the odour and the effects of passive smoking for children and colleagues.

Legal framework

  • health and safety regulations and the Welfare Requirements of the EYFS.
  • The Smoke-free (Premises and Enforcement) Regulations 2006
  • The Smoke-free (Signs) Regulations 2007
